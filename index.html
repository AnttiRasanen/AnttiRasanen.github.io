Eagle Creek Golf Club

By Steve Stevenson

Eagle Creek Golf Club is an eighteen-hole golf course located in Orlando, Florida. The course has a par of 73 and measures 7,198 yards (6,582 m), containing five par-5 holes and over ninety bunkers. The course is public and includes a 14,000-square-foot (1,300 m2) clubhouse with a pro shop as well as a 120-seat restaurant.

Sedgeford Hall Portrait

By Steve Stevenson

The "Sedgeford Hall Portrait" (location: King's Lynn Town Hall, Norfolk, UK), is an oil on canvas portrait in the American School by an unknown artist circa 1830. It depicts Pe-o-ka, wife of the Seminole chief, Osceola, and their son. It was once believed by many to be a portrait painted from life of Pocahontas and her son, Thomas Rolfe.
